Who is Dayonikin?

Dayonikin, also known as Dayon, is a Canadian artist and producer born April 12, 2005, from Kitchener, Ontario. His work spans underground hip-hop, experimental rap, and emotionally driven soundscapes, exploring his identity, pressure, and perceptions.

MIND OVER MATTER

MIND OVER MATTER is Dayon's debut full-length album, blending experimental rap, emo rap, trap, boom bap, and R&B into a raw and emotionally driven body of work centered on perseverance and strength.

A LIVING NIGHTMARE

A LIVING NIGHTMARE is a collaborative album with producer AVI Christian, released through HXS Music Official, exploring fear, self-doubt, and mental confinement.

MY DARK DELUSIONS

MY DARK DELUSIONS is a 35-track descent into paranoia, emotional instability, and mental overload, documenting chaos as it unfolds.
