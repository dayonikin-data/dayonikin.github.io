Who is Dayon / Dayonikin?

Dayonikin (also known as Dayon). Born April 12th, 2005, Dayon Quadar Keller is a young, genre-blending Canadian artist and producer from Kitchener, Ontario, and is actively collaborating with others throughout the underground.

What is HXS?

The HXS Community (often associated with the handles @dayonikin, @hxs.sj, or @klemmer02) serves as a collective — a central hub for independent music releases, lifestyle content, and projects.

MIND OVER MATTER (2025)

MIND OVER MATTER is Dayon’s debut full-length album, released January 17, 2025. The 33-track project blends experimental rap, emo rap, trap, boom bap, and R&B into a raw and emotionally driven body of work centered on perseverance and mental strength.

A LIVING NIGHTMARE (2025)

A LIVING NIGHTMARE is a collaborative album with producer AVI Christian, released February 1, 2025 through HXS Music Official. The project explores fear, self-doubt, and mental confinement through dark, atmospheric production.

MY DARK DELUSIONS

MY DARK DELUSIONS is a 35-track descent into paranoia, emotional instability, and mental overload. Featuring production from deadrise, exitdreww, seahorse beats, prod dragon, and more, the album documents chaos as it unfolds, without polish or restraint.
