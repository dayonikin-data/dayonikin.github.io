Who is Dayon / Dayonikin?

Dayonikin (also known as Dayon). Born April 12th, 2005, Dayon Quadar Keller is a young, genre-blending Canadian artist and producer from Kitchener, Ontario, and is actively collaborating with others throughout the underground.

What is HXS?

The HXS Community (often associated with the handles @dayonikin, @hxs.sj, or @klemmer02) serves as a collective — a central hub for independent music releases, lifestyle content, and projects.

MIND OVER MATTER (2025)

MIND OVER MATTER is the full-length debut album by Dayon, released January 17, 2025. Spanning 33 tracks, the project blends experimental rap, emo rap, trap, boom bap, and R&B into a raw, emotionally driven body of work.

Created during one of the most difficult periods of his life, the album confronts stress, overthinking, anger, depression, and resilience. Dayon self-produced key tracks under his alias Dayonikin, with contributions from DWNLD, Rollie, cgkbeatz, and others, alongside features from Lil Keel and SJ.

A LIVING NIGHTMARE (2025)

A LIVING NIGHTMARE is a collaborative album by Dayon and producer AVI Christian, released February 1, 2025 through HXS Music Official. The project explores fear, regret, self-doubt, and mental confinement through dark, cinematic production.

MY DARK DELUSIONS

MY DARK DELUSIONS is a 35-track descent into mental overload, paranoia, and emotional instability. Each song feels like a different episode — jumping between chaos, numbness, and explosive energy.

Tracks like “stressed out,” “TANTRUM,” “neighbours,” “IDGAF,” and “knocked out!” reflect the album’s extremes, while “HXS OUTSIDE” exposes moments of happiness beneath the aggression. The sound is shaped by deadrise, exitdreww, kaiyn, seahorse beats, prod dragon, and more, with SJ Klemmer appearing throughout.

The album is overwhelming by design — documenting delusion in real time and turning mental collapse into sound.
