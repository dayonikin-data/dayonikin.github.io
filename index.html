Dayonikin

Dayonikin (also known as Dayon; born Dayon Quadar Keller) is a Canadian artist exploring his identity, form, and digital expression.

His work spans visual art, conceptual design, and emerging digital spaces, often focusing on minimalism, presence, and self-documentation.

Through evolving online archives and experimental projects, Dayonikin investigates how identity exists within technology, memory, and time.
