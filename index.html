Who is Dayon / Dayonikin?

Dayonikin (also known as Dayon). Born April 12th, 2005, Dayon Quadar Keller is a genre-blending Canadian artist and producer from Kitchener, Ontario, actively collaborating throughout the underground.

MIND OVER MATTER (2025)

MIND OVER MATTER is Dayon’s debut full-length album, released January 17, 2025. The 33-track project blends experimental rap, emo rap, trap, boom bap, and R&B into a raw and emotionally driven body of work centered on perseverance and strength.

A LIVING NIGHTMARE (2025)

A LIVING NIGHTMARE is a collaborative album with producer AVI Christian, released February 1, 2025 through HXS Music Official. The project explores fear, self-doubt, and mental confinement through dark, atmospheric production.

MY DARK DELUSIONS (2026)

MY DARK DELUSIONS is a 35-track descent into paranoia, emotional instability, and mental overload. Featuring production from deadrise, exitdreww, seahorse beats, prod dragon, and more, the album documents chaos as it unfolds.
